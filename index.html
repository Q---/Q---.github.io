Welcome to my Modeling and Simulation lab.

This is a cetralized repository of the various physics and math projects I work on. Lately, my goal has been to build accurate physical simulations of pendulums, with the end goal of a physically accurate rope. (i.e. one with no energy loss).

Most, if not all, literature you can find on the subject (And by literature, I mean game development discussions on the web) use inaccurate, if computationally inexpensive tricks. Furthermore, the state of the rope is usually in Cartesian, rather than generalized, coordinates.
