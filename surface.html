Spline Surface

This applet allows one to interactively define spline surfaces that may fit arbitrary electron density maps (in the .mrc format). A scoring function is provided, which tracks the current R^2 score of the chosen fit. This is calculated from the sum of the minimum squared distance of every density point to the surface.

To increase the ease of manually defining splines, this applet automatically fits the chosen density map to the x-z plane. This is done using a "plane of best fit" iterative optimization algorithm, that was previously discussed.
